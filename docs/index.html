What We Do


Acerta offers a SaaS platform that uses machine learning to provide real-time malfunction detection and failure prediction. Our proprietary state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms allow our clients to access the full potential of their collected data.

The platform learns the normal behavior of the tested system and the complex correlations between data streams, and automatically detects anomalies in real-time. It assists engineers in identifying the root cause and provides them with actionable insight, thus saving millions of dollars in warranty claims, brand value, manufacturing down time, engineering resources, and legal expenses.
